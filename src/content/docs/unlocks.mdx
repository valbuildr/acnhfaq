## Nook Phone Unlocks

|Item|Unlock Criteria|
|-|-|
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconPhoto" /><br/>**Camera**                                                | Unlocked by default.<br/><br/>• **Pro Camera** - 1,500 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Unlocks the first person, tripod, and bird's eye view camera views. Also unlocks several new camera filters. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconBook2" /><br/>**Critterpedia**                                          | Donate a critter to Tom Nook. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconRecipe" /><LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconRecipePlus" /><br/>**DIY Recipes** | Complete Tom Nook's DIY workshop.<br/><br/>• **DIY Recipes+** - 2,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Allows you to receive cooking DIY recipes and cook. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconCatalogue" /><br/>**Nook Shopping**                                     | After purchasing 100 items from “Nook Shopping” in the Nook Stop, you will get a prompt that you unlocked the app. You can track your progress with the “Shop to it” Nook Miles achievement. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconLicense" /><br/>**Island Designer**                                     | Complete Project K and redeem the purchases from the Nook Stop (“Redeem Nook Miles”). Preset path permits cost 2,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" />, the Custom Path permit costs 2,300 <ButtonIcon button="NM" />, and the waterscaping and cliff-construction permits cost 6,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /> each. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconMyDesign" /><br/>**Custom Designs**                                     | Unlocked by default.<br/><br/>• **Pro Editor** - 800 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Unlocks the use of Pro Designs.<br/>• **Pro Editor+** - 2,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Unlocks 50 additional custom design slots for both normal and pro designs. Also unlocks new pro custom design types.<br/>• **Patterns+** - 1,800 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Allows the use of Sable patterns as custom designs.<br/>• **Portal** - 300 <ButtonIcon button="NM" /><br/>Allows remote access to the Custom Design Kiosk. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconMap" /><br/>**Map**                                                     | Unlocked by default. This app shows the map of the island. Only works on main island. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconClientList" /><br/>**Happy Home Network**                               | <Badge text="DLC ONLY" variant="danger" size="medium" /><br/>Unlocked by building your second vacation home on Paradise Planning.<br/><br/>• **Showroom** - Complete 13 vacation homes.<br/>Unlocks online features, including sharing home showcases and following online profiles. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconStudy" /><br/>**Room Sketch**                                           | <Badge text="DLC ONLY" variant="danger" size="medium" /><br/>Unlocked by visiting Paradise Planning on 11 different days and learn all Soundscapes. |<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconProfile" /><br/>**Passport**                                            | Unlocked by default. This app displays Resident information. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconBestFriend" /><br/>**Best Friends List**                                | Talk to Orville about playing with others. This app displays the list of friends and best friends. |
|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconChatLog" /><br/>**Chat Log**                                            | Unlocked by default. This app provides a log of recent chats. |

|<LocalIcon icon="DeviceIconRescue" /><br/><b><ButtonIcon button="Ex" /> Rescue Service</b>      | Unlocked by default. This app allows the player to teleport to pre-determined locations in exchange for miles if they are ever stuck. |

## Villager Plot Unlocks

| Plot Number | Unlock Criteria                                                       |
|-------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **1 and 2** | Create your island!                                                   |
| **3 to 5**  | Unlock the Museum Tent, build Nook's Cranny, build your first bridge. |
| **6**       | Unlock and build the Campsite.                                        |
| **7 to 10** | Speak to Tom Nook about purchasing land.                              |

## Building Unlocks

<Aside type="note">
  As of `Ver. 2.0.0` the Museum Art Wing is automatically unlocked once you upgrade it from a tent. Those without the Art wing will still have to meet the requirements to upgrade.
</Aside>

|Building|Unlock Criteria|
|:-:|-|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconMuseum00" /><br/>**Museum Tent**| Donate 5 bugs or fish to Tom Nook. It will open the day after you place the plot marker.|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconMuseum01" /><br/>**Museum**| Donate 15 bugs, fish, or fossils to Blathers.|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconMuseum02" /><br/>**Museum Art Wing**| Total 60 donations to Blathers (40 on top of the 20 donated to unlock the previous two stages).|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconMuseum02" /><br/>**The Roost**| Donate at least one of each category of museum collection (insects, fish, fossils, art and sea creatures).|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconShop01" /><br/>**Nook's Cranny <br/>(Stage 1)**| Pay off your initial Nook Miles debt and upgrade your tent into a house.|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconShop02" /><br/>**Nook's Cranny <br/>(Stage 2)**| 30 days must have passed since Nook's Cranny was built. You must have made 200,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /> worth of transactions (buying and/or selling) at the shop, and Mabel must have visited! Once all three requirements are fulfilled, the upgrade is unlocked.|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconTailor00" /><br/>**Able Sisters**| Buy at least one item from Mabel's stand in front of Residents Services on **two** different visits. She will ask to set up her shop on the third visit.| 
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconOffice00" /><br/>**Resident Services Building**| Pay your Nook Miles debt, give 5 critters to Tom Nook, build Blather's tent, build the first bridge, build Nook's Cranny, and complete furnishing the 3 plots.|
| <LocalIcon icon="BuildingIconCamp00" /><br/>**Campsite**| Upgrade Resident Services into a **building** and talk to Tom Nook, who will have you craft and place the Campground Construction Kit.|

| **Basement Addition**     | 2,498,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" />                                                                                                                               | • Basement (10x6 grid)<br/>• 800 additional storage slots (1600 total) <br/>• Free exterior renovations (once per day)                                                                       |
| **Storage Upgrade**       | 500,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /><br/>700,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /><br/>900,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /><br/>1,200,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /> | • 800 additional storage slots (2400 total)<br/>• 800 additional storage slots (3200 total)<br/>• 800 additional storage slots (4000 total)<br/>• 1000 additional storage slots (5000 total) |

In total, a **fully upgraded home** will cost you 5,696,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /> and 5,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" />.
A fully upgraded home **with all additional storage upgrades** will cost you 8,996,000 <ButtonIcon button="Bells" /> and 5,000 <ButtonIcon button="NM" />.

<Aside type="note">
  - Paying off the previous fee unlocks each subsequent house upgrade.
  - Free exterior renovations are only unlocked after paying the final loan in full.
</Aside>

  Golden tools can still break, but have a considerably higher durability. Visit https://cestislife.github.io/img/tools.png for tool durability.
