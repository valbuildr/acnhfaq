Event NPCs are the star of their respective events, being both the mascot as well as the organiser. They are the source of most, if not all of the event related rewards you can receive during their events. 

## <ExternalIcon icon="pck" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Pav√©

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpPck" />

- First encounter on [Festivale](/events/#festivale-mardi-gras).
- Explains and hosts Festivale, and gives you the Rainbow Feather DIY, along with the Festivale Float.

## <ExternalIcon icon="pyn" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Zipper T. Bunny

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpPyn" />

- First encounter at the beginning of [Bunny Day](/events#bunny-day-easter) event.
- Explains and hosts Bunny Day.

## <ExternalIcon icon="alw" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> <ExternalIcon icon="alp" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Reese and Cyrus

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpAlw" />
<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpAlp" />

- First encounter during the [Wedding event](/events#wedding-season).
- Appear on Harv's island, the player can assist them in taking wedding photos in exchange for special furniture.

## <ExternalIcon icon="xct" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Rover

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpXct" />

- First encounter during [May Day](/events#may-day-labour-day) mystery tour event.
- At the end of the maze, the player will get to speak with Rover and is gifted the "Rover's briefcase."

## <ExternalIcon icon="pkn" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Jack

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpPkn" />

- First encounter during [Halloween](/events#halloween).
- Gifts various rewards in exchange for candies and lollipops.

## <ExternalIcon icon="tuk" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Franklin

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpTuk" />

- First encounter during [Turkey Day](/events#turkey-day-thanksgiving).
- Gifts various rewards in exchange for needed ingredients.

## <ExternalIcon icon="rei" category="NpcIcon" class="h-10" /> Jingle

<FloatRightPoster poster="PosterNpcSpRei" />

- First encounter during [Toy Day](/events#toy-day-christmas).
- Hosts the event and rewards players with Toy Day related DIYs and furniture.
